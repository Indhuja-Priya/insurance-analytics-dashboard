
# 🏢 XYZ Insurance — Claims & Policy Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze insurance policy performance, claims data, and customer demographics for a fictional insurance company (XYZ Insurance Pvt. Ltd.).

## 📊 Overview

This dashboard helps stakeholders answer key business questions:
- What is the total premium, coverage, and claim amount across all policies?
- Which policy type generates the highest premium revenue?
- How many policies are active vs. inactive?
- How does claim amount vary across different age groups?
- What is the claim status breakdown (Pending / Rejected / Settled) by policy type?

## 🎯 Key Insights

- **Total Premium Amount:** 5.98M | **Total Coverage Amount:** 600.55M | **Total Claim Amount:** 16.91M
- **Travel** policies generate the highest premium amount (2.5M), followed by Health (1.2M)
- **41.87%** of policies are Active vs **58.13%** Inactive
- Claim amounts are highest among **Adults**, decreasing sharply for **Elder** and **Young Adults**
- Across all policy types, **Rejected claims (26.15M)** outweigh **Settled claims (20.29M)**, highlighting a potential area for process improvement

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — dashboard design and data modeling
- **DAX** — calculated measures (totals, percentages)
- **Power Query** — data cleaning and transformation
- **Data Visualization** — KPI cards, donut chart, area chart, bar chart, matrix table
- **Interactive Filters** — slice by Policy Number, Claim Number, and Customer ID

## 📁 Dashboard Pages

1. **Page 1 — Overview:** KPI summary, claims by status, premium by policy type, active/inactive split, claim amount by age group, policy-wise claim status table
2. **Page 2 — Raw Data View:** Full underlying dataset for transparency
